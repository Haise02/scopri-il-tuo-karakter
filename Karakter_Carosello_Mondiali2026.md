## Slide 1/8 — Copertina

```
High-impact sports broadcast carousel slide, 4:5 vertical, 1080x1350px.
Style: television sports-graphics poster. Aggressive, punchy, scroll-stopping.

PHOTO (full bleed, edge to edge): a vast floodlit football stadium at night, shot from
pitch level. Fine rain slashing through the warm amber floodlight beams. The stands are
a packed blur. In the mid-ground, a silver-haired European manager in his sixties, dark
technical coat, arms folded, stands alone at the edge of the technical area, watching.
He is small in the frame — the stadium dwarfs him. Photojournalistic, shot on Canon EOS
R3 at 35mm f/1.4, available light, warm amber highlights against cold navy shadows.
Visible film grain, real rain, wet grass sheen. Not CGI, not airbrushed, no HDR.
Graded so the shadows crush to navy #0B1F3A and the deepest areas to #060E1A.

CHAPTER NUMBER: "01" set in ENORMOUS ultra-bold condensed sans, OUTLINE ONLY (3px lime
#A3E635 stroke, transparent fill), top-left, bleeding off the left edge of the canvas,
at 60% opacity. Purely graphic.

SIGNATURE ELEMENT — the lime band:
A thick parallelogram band of solid lime #A3E635, slanted at 4 degrees, running edge to
edge across the lower-middle of the canvas, with a hard drop shadow beneath it so it
floats above the photo. Inside the band, the headline set in NAVY #0B1F3A — inverted
contrast.

TYPOGRAPHY: ULTRA-BOLD CONDENSED SANS-SERIF, OBLIQUE / ITALIC — Druk Condensed Bold
Italic, Anton italic, or Knockout oblique. Uppercase, extremely tight leading, very
heavy weight, slanted to match the band. Absolutely NOT a serif. Never Playfair,
never Didot.

Inside the lime band, two lines, navy #0B1F3A:
"LA VERA NOVITÀ DEI MONDIALI 2026"
"NON SONO LE 48 SQUADRE."

Directly BELOW the band, on the photo, one line in ultra-bold condensed oblique,
white #FFFFFF, medium size, with a hard drop shadow:
"È CHI SIEDE IN PANCHINA E NON ALLENA I PIEDI."

FURNITURE:
- Top-right: small "KARAKTER" logo lockup, white.
- Bottom-left: a thin white chalk line runs horizontally across the frame, like a pitch
  marking, at 40% opacity.
- Bottom-right: "01 / 08" in white monospace, small, letter-spaced.
- Bottom-centre: "SWIPE ≫" in small bold condensed white caps, 60% opacity.

No national flags, no team crests, no sponsor logos. No smiling. No plastic skin.
No serif typography except the logo.
```
